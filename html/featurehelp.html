If the feature is a song or hymn it should be available from the drop-down list. If it is not on the list, or is not a song or hymn then enter the details in the box below the drop-down list. If a song or hymn is not available on the drop-down list you can still use it, but please take the trouble to add it to the database using the Song Adder frame at the bottom right.
You can also add some notes if you wish to (for example) describe the sequence of the song, or tell your projectionist to be ready to switch songs quickly.
